# Kinetic Gain Embedded Tie-Back

`employee-ai-acceptable-use-board` is the public proof surface for a deeper embedded product pattern: AI policy evidence, exception routing, training readiness, and rollout-safe governance inside enterprise workforce workflows.

## Embedded angle

- ingest policy, acknowledgment, and exception snapshots from employee systems and adjacent HR/security tooling
- surface missing attestations, stale approvals, training blockers, and manager exception debt before AI rollouts go broad
- attach evidence packets and escalation history to each policy lane
- expose review-safe dashboards for security, HR, legal, and enablement stakeholders
